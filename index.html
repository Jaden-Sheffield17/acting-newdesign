ACTING tECHNIQUES: a HOW-TO ON USING VARIOUS ACTING METHODS

What is an acting technique?

An acting technique is a way of channeling emotions, thoughts, or actions through yourself to increase the authenticity of the role you play. You may recall an emotional memory for example to induce crying for a sad scene.

Why are acting techniques important?

Acting techniques provide a varying level of qualilty in the acting you do. Depending on the acting technique you utilize and your ability to effectively perform that technique, you could either ruin or greatly enhance your piece.

How many acting techniques are there?

There's many individual acting methods, however we will cover only three. You can even invent your own acting method, or spin off of another one. The beauty of acting is that it's all personalized and never the same.
